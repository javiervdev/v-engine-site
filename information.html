WHAT IS V-ENGINE?

The purpose of V-ENGINE is to provide a powerful and lightweight 3D graphics engine for modern web browsers. It allows developers and artists to create real-time 3D experiences, interactive demos, and full-featured web-based games directly in the browser — with no plugins required.

We welcome the following:

  • –Developers who want to build interactive 3D applications
  • –Artists who want to visualize their models in real-time
  • –People who want to learn about rendering and game engine design
  • –Web developers who want to integrate 3D graphics into their projects
  • –Enthusiasts who want to experiment with real-time physics, lighting, and shaders
